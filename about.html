about liz ward

Liz Ward is a UK-based knitting and crochet designer with over a decade of experience turning yarn into imaginative, accessible creations. Based in historic York, Liz has worked with leading British craft magazines and book publishers, building a reputation for her easy-to-follow patterns that draw inspiration from geek culture, TV, and film.

Her designs are known for blending creativity with clarity, making them perfect for beginners and seasoned crafters alike. Alongside her design work, Liz is also an experienced layout photographer for craft publications and regularly produces video tutorials to help others bring patterns to life.

Liz is the creator and host of the Yarn Waffle YouTube channel, where she hosts a monthly podcast and the fan-favorite Spin to Knit series.
